CSE375 Track 1:
Scaffold Safety Knowledge Quiz
脚手架安全理论测验

Time Limit (限时): 15 Minutes

Total Score (总分): 100 Points

Section A: Multiple Choice (单选题)
1. 根据法定规例，在正常情况下，脚手架必须多久由“合资格人员”检查一次？ According to statutory regulations, how often must a scaffold be inspected by a "Competent Person" under normal conditions?
2. 合资格人员在完成脚手架检查后，必须签署并展示哪份法定表格？ Which statutory form must be signed and displayed by the Competent Person after a successful scaffold inspection?
3. 除了定期检查外，在什么情况下必须立即对脚手架进行重新检查？ Besides the regular scheduled intervals, under what circumstances MUST a scaffold be immediately re-inspected?
4. 在搭建或拆卸脚手架时，搭棚工必须将全身式安全带的挂绳锚固在哪里？ When erecting or dismantling a scaffold, where MUST a scaffolder anchor the lanyard of their full-body safety harness?
5. 在脚手架工作平台上安装踢脚板的主要目的是什么？ What is the primary purpose of toe-boards installed on a scaffold working platform?
6. 以下哪项是承重竹竿被严格要求报废的标准？ Which of the following is a strict condemnation criterion for a bamboo pole intended for structural use?
7. 根据标准行业惯例，应使用什么材料来绑扎和固定竹脚手架？ According to standard industry practice, what material should be used for tying and securing bamboo scaffolds?
8. 在金属管件脚手架系统中，交叉支撑的关键作用是什么？ In a metal tubular scaffold system, what is the critical function of cross bracing?
9. 当金属脚手架搭建在不平整或松软的地面上时，底座或可调底托下方必须放置什么？ What must be placed under the base plates or adjustable jacks of a metal scaffold when erected on uneven or soft ground?
10. 一线安全主管如何准确核查特定脚手架平台的安全工作载荷/SWL？ How can a frontline safety supervisor accurately verify the Safe Working Load (SWL) of a specific scaffold platform?
Section B: Short Answer (简答题)
11. 简述在劳工处指引下，脚手架检查的“合资格人员”需要具备什么条件？ Briefly define the qualifications of a "Competent Person" for scaffold inspection under the Labour Department guidelines.
12. 解释为什么法律要求高空作业的搭棚工必须使用全身式安全带，而不是简单的安全腰带。 Explain why a full-body harness is legally required for scaffolders working at height, as opposed to a simple safety belt.
13. 什么是竹脚手架中的“连墙件/拉潃”？为什么它们在结构上至关重要？ What are "tie-backs" in bamboo scaffolding, and why are they structurally critical?
14. 在对金属脚手架进行目视检查时，列出两个表明金属扣件应报废并停止使用的视觉迹象。 During a visual inspection of a metal scaffold, list two visible signs that indicate a metal coupler should be condemned and removed from service.
15. 作为一线主管，如果您发现工人将一大堆沉重的混凝土砖块堆放在标准的“通道脚手架平台”上，您应立即采取什么行动？为什么？ If you, as a frontline supervisor, spot workers stacking a large pile of heavy concrete bricks on a standard "access scaffold platform", what immediate action should you take and why?
