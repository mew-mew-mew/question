CSE375 Track 1:
Scaffold Safety Knowledge Quiz
脚手架安全理论测验

Time Limit (限时): 15 Minutes

Total Score (总分): 100 Points

15道单项选择题 (Multiple Choice Questions)
1. 根据法定规例，在正常情况下，脚手架必须多久由“合资格人员”检查一次？ According to statutory regulations, how often must a scaffold be inspected by a "Competent Person" under normal conditions?
2. 合资格人员在完成脚手架检查后，必须签署并展示哪份法定表格？ Which statutory form must be signed and displayed by the Competent Person after a successful scaffold inspection?
3. 除了定期检查外，在什么情况下必须立即对脚手架进行重新检查？ Besides the regular scheduled intervals, under what circumstances MUST a scaffold be immediately re-inspected?
4. 在搭建或拆卸脚手架时，搭棚工必须将全身式安全带的挂绳锚固在哪里？ When erecting or dismantling a scaffold, where MUST a scaffolder anchor the lanyard of their full-body safety harness?
5. 在脚手架工作平台上安装踢脚板的主要目的是什么？ What is the primary purpose of toe-boards installed on a scaffold working platform?
6. 以下哪项是承重竹竿被严格要求报废的标准？ Which of the following is a strict condemnation criterion for a bamboo pole intended for structural use?
7. 根据标准行业惯例，应使用什么材料来绑扎和固定竹脚手架？ According to standard industry practice, what material should be used for tying and securing bamboo scaffolds?
8. 在竹脚手架中，“连墙件/拉潃”的主要结构功能是什么？ In bamboo scaffolding, what is the primary structural function of "tie-backs"?
9. 在金属管件脚手架系统中，交叉支撑的关键作用是什么？ In a metal tubular scaffold system, what is the critical function of cross bracing?
10. 当金属脚手架搭建在不平整或松软的地面上时，底座或可调底托下方必须放置什么？ What must be placed under the base plates or adjustable jacks of a metal scaffold when erected on uneven or soft ground?
11. 在目视检查中，以下哪种情况表明金属扣件应被报废并停止使用？ During a visual inspection, which of the following indicates that a metal coupler should be CONDEMNED and removed from service?
12. 一线安全主管如何准确核查特定脚手架平台的安全工作载荷/SWL？ How can a frontline safety supervisor accurately verify the Safe Working Load (SWL) of a specific scaffold platform?
13. 关于脚手架工作平台上的跳板，安全要求是什么？ What is the safety requirement regarding the planks on a scaffold working platform?
14. 如果工人在脚手架上使用安全腰带代替全身式安全带进行防坠落保护，坠落时的主要危险是什么？ If a worker uses a safety belt instead of a full-body harness for fall protection on a scaffold, what is the primary danger in the event of a fall?
15. 作为主管，如果您观察到工人在未经适当授权的情况下修改或拆除脚手架结构的某一部分（例如拆除连墙件），您必须立即采取什么行动？ As a supervisor, what immediate action MUST you take if you observe workers modifying or removing part of a scaffold structure without proper authorization?
